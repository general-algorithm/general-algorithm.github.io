EmbodiedPilot-1: Imagine What Will Happen Before Taking Drive Actions

Last updated at July 27, 2024.

Our approach emulates human-like behavior, which imagines what will happen in future scenarios before taking drive actions. Instead of only predicting labeled agents in supervised learning methods, we predict the whole world for trajectory planning.
